About HoopHer

Welcome to HoopHer, the ultimate destination for women's basketball enthusiasts. Whether you're a lifelong fan or just discovering the game, we're here to bring you closer to the action. From celebrating the athletes who inspire us to keeping you updated on all the latest news, HoopHer is dedicated to elevating women's basketball and highlighting its incredible talent.

Join us as we champion the sport and its stars, share stories that matter, and foster a community where women's basketball is front and center!

What We Do

At HoopHer, we're committed to providing a comprehensive experience for women's basketball fans, players, and supporters. Here's what we bring to the table:

  • Latest News and Updates: Stay in the loop with the most current happenings in the world of women's basketball. From game highlights to team updates, we've got you covered.
  • Spotlights: Discover the stories behind players and teams. We feature in-depth profiles of all things women's basketball and even highlight in-game plays.
  • Merch: Rep your women's basketball merch, created by us, and support the sport we all love while looking good while doing it.
  • Community Engagement: Join a passionate community of women's basketball supporters. Share your thoughts, connect with fellow fans, and get involved in events and discussions.
